Stop Trying to Get Good Grades

September 10, 2020

Getting good grades is a trap.

You should stop trying to get good grades for three reasons:

  • Nobody cares about them
  • They make you dumber
  • They set you up for failure

School and reality are two separate universes. They each have their own laws of physics. Rules that work in real life don't work in school.

In fact, the laws of school are opposite to the laws of reality. What makes you win in school makes you lose in life.

In school, you win by...

  • Avoiding failure
  • Following instructions
  • Playing it safe

The only skill kids learn in school is test-taking. They don't "learn how to learn" - they learn how to play a game.

That's like a kid learning Monopoly for 16 years then expected to be a real estate developer.

And when you learn the grade game, you play the game of life by the wrong rules.
